Hybrid Strength Playbook

(Hyrox Edition)

This guide will help you balance strength, endurance, and Hyrox-style workouts so you can build muscle while improving conditioning.

This plan focuses on key Hyrox movements such as sled pushes, rowing, wall balls, and running, ensuring you develop the strength, speed, and endurance needed to start training a hybrid style.

How to Balance Strength & Cardio (Without Overtraining)

  • ✅ Prioritise Strength First – Lift heavy first, then do conditioning.
  • ✅ Use Functional Cardio – Rowing, SkiErg, sleds, and running instead of endless steady-state cardio.
  • ✅ Increase Volume Gradually – Build endurance by gradually adding reps and rounds.
  • ✅ Recover Properly – Use active mobility drills to avoid burnout and keep things fresh.

3-Day High-Volume Hybrid Training Plan

This plan includes strength, Hyrox-style circuits, and endurance training with sets, reps, and distances for each station.

Day 1: Strength & Power + Hyrox Circuit

Strength Focus:

  • Back Squats – 5 sets of 5 reps (heavy load, focus on strength)
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Pull-Ups (Assisted if needed) – 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Farmer’s Carry – 3 rounds of 30 meters, heavy dumbbells

Hyrox Circuit (Complete 3 Rounds):

  • 400m Run (at decent pace)
  • 25 Wall Balls (8kg for men, 6kg for women)
  • 10 Burpees to Box Jumps (20-inch box)
  • 200m Row (maintain sub-2:10 pace)

Day 2: Endurance + Functional Circuit

Main Workout (Complete 5 Rounds):

  • 500m Row (race effort pace/fast pace)
  • 15 Kettlebell Swings (24kg for men, 16kg for women)
  • 10 Dumbbell Thrusters (2x15kg for men, 2x10kg for women)
  • 2 lengths Sled Pushes (12.5m per/heavy weight)
  • 25m Walking Lunges (Bodyweight or Weighted)

Day 3: Strength & Functional Hybrid

Strength Focus:

  • Overhead Press – 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats – 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • SkiErg – 250m sprints x 3 (target pace: sub-1:45/500m)

Hyrox Circuit (Complete 4 Rounds):

  • 300m RowErg (maintain steady pace)
  • 15 Burpee Broad Jumps (max effort distance per jump)
  • 10 Sandbag Lunges (25kg for men, 15kg for women)
  • 10 Box Step-Ups with Dumbbells (controlled, steady pace)
