5-Step Process Killer

0 What process are we killing today?

1 Make your requirements less dumb

Who originally wrote the current requirements? Are they actually knowledgeable, or is it “how we’ve always done it”?



2 Work very hard to delete the part or process

If you had a magic wand and could delete anything without consequence, what would you delete first?



3 Simplify the process or part. Requirements should come from person not from the department

What is the real human need here? What should the process actually achieve in the simplest possible way?



4 Accelerate cycle times

How can we make this 10× faster? From days → hours → minutes → seconds?



5 Automate

Now that it’s simple and fast, what can be fully automated with almost no risk?
