How to Use Your Trip Planner

Getting Started with Triply

Triply makes trip planning simple and fun. Follow these steps to create, manage, and enjoy your travel itinerary.

1. Create Your Trip

On the Planner Page

  • Trip Title: Give your trip a name (e.g., "Summer Adventure").
  • Start Date: Pick your departure date.
  • Destinations: List your stops as "City/Airport" (e.g., "London/GTW, Doha/DOH").
  • Days per Stop: Enter days for each stop (e.g., "0, 2" - 0 for transit).
  • Interests: Add what you love (e.g., "sightseeing, food").
  • Click Create Trip to build your plan.

Tip: Use "0" days for transit stops where you won’t stay overnight.

2. Manage Your Itinerary

On the Plan Page

  • Summary: See all stops with flight and hotel details.
  • Edit a Stop: Click "…" to open the overlay, update dates, flights, hotels, or notes, then click Save.
  • Add a Stop: Click Add Stop to insert a new location.
  • Delete a Stop: In the overlay, click Delete.
  • Save Changes: Hit Save Changes to update your plan.

3. Visualize Your Trip

The Timeline shows your journey:

  • Circles: Each stop, colored by status (past, current, booked).
  • Lines: Travel between stops.
  • Bars: Days spent at each stop.
  • Click a circle to edit that stop.

4. Import or Export Your Plan

  • Export: Click Export Data to download your trip as a JSON file.
  • Import: On either page, click Import Data, select your JSON file, and load your trip.
  • Clear Data: Reset everything with Clear Data.
