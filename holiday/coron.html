Coron Wonders 🌊

Days 16-18: Coron (April 7-9) 🏞️

Transportation: On April 7, van from El Nido to Puerto Princesa (~4 hrs), then to Busuanga (~2 hrs), and van to Coron (~1 hr). All land vibes! 🚐

Stay: Club Paradise Resort or Coron Bayview Resort - close to the action! 🌴

Activities: Three nights (April 7-8):

  • Kayangan Lake - crystal clear beauty 🏊
  • Barracuda Lake - dive in! 🤿
  • Maquinit Hot Spring - warm relaxation ♨️
  • Shipwreck snorkeling - history underwater 🚢
  • Drone shots - lakes & scenery 📸 (check rules!)

Unexpected Fun: Shipwrecks add a cool historical twist to your beach time! ✨

Festival Alert! 🎉 Post-Holy Week calm (April 5 was Easter), but a small saint’s day fiesta might pop up—check with locals near Kayangan Lake! 🎶
