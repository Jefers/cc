User Guide 📖

Welcome to Your Philippine Adventure Planner! 🌴

This guide will help you use our handy travel planning system. Whether you’re booking flights, tracking hotels, or managing costs, it’s all here—easy and stress-free!

Getting Started

Start at the main page. You’ll see links to all the tools:

  • Hotel Tracker 🏨: Add and manage hotel bookings.
  • Real-Time Tracker ✈️: Track flight details.
  • Budget Tracker 💸: Keep an eye on your spending.
  • Booking Guide 📋: Tips for booking your trip.

How to Use Each Tool

Adding a Hotel 🏨

Go to "Hotel Tracker." For each location (e.g., Manila):

  1. Enter the hotel name, booking ID, arrival time, Google Plus Code (copy from Google Maps), and notes.
  2. Click "Confirm Booking" to save it.
  3. It’ll show up in the "Confirmed Hotel List" with options to edit, delete, or add to Google Calendar.
Tracking a Flight ✈️

Go to "Real-Time Tracker." For each flight (e.g., Manila to Bohol):

  1. Enter the flight number, departure, and arrival times.
  2. Click "Confirm Booking" to save it.
  3. See it in the "Confirmed Real-Time List" with edit, delete, or Google Calendar options.
Managing Your Budget 💸

Go to "Budget Tracker."

  1. Enter costs for bookings (flights, hotels) and extras (food, taxis).
  2. Watch the "Running Total" update automatically.
  3. Data saves locally—no edit/delete needed, just update the numbers.

Editing or Deleting Entries

Made a mistake? No worries!

  • Edit: Click "Edit" on a hotel or flight, update the fields, and re-confirm.
  • Delete: Click "Delete," confirm the prompt, and it’s gone.

Sharing Your Plans

Back on the main page:

  1. Click "Export All Data" to download a travel-data.json file with all your bookings.
  2. Email it to your travel buddy!
  3. They can upload it by selecting the file in "Import All Data" on their device.

Adding Photos 📸

Want to spice up your itinerary pages (e.g., Manila)?

  1. Upload a photo to Google Drive, set it to "Anyone with the link," and copy the link.
  2. Edit the HTML file (e.g., manila.html) to add: <img src="https://drive.google.com/uc?export=view&id=YOUR_FILE_ID" alt="Manila">
  3. Replace YOUR_FILE_ID with the ID from your link.
