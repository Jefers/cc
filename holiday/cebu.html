Cebu City Vibes 🎉

Days 8-11: Cebu (March 30 - April 2) 🌇

Transportation: Fly from Tagbilaran to Cebu City (~30 min, Flight 2) on March 30. Book at Philippine Airlines ✈️

Stay: Shangri-La Mactan or Cebu City Marriott Hotel - city & beach combo! 🏖️🏙️

Activities: Four nights (March 30-April 1):

  • Magellan's Cross - history vibes ✝️
  • Fort San Pedro - old-school fortress 🏰
  • Basilica Minore del Santo Niño - cultural gem ⛪
  • Moalboal diving - underwater fun 🤿

Drone Tip: Check local rules for epic shots! 📸

Unexpected Fun: Cebu’s urban-beach mix is a cool surprise! 🌟
