El Nido Paradise 🏝️

Days 12-15: El Nido (April 3-6) 🌊

Transportation: Fly from Cebu City to Puerto Princesa (~1 hr 15 min, Flight 3) on April 3, then 4-hr van to El Nido. Book at Philippine Airlines ✈️

Stay: El Nido Resort or Lagen Island Resort - luxury with a view! 🌴

Activities: Four nights (April 3-5):

  • Island hopping: Big Lagoon, Small Lagoon, Matinloc Shrine 🛶
  • Snorkeling - underwater wonders 🤿
  • Kayaking - paddle through paradise 🚣
  • Drone shots - epic cliffs & waters 📸 (check rules!)

Unexpected Fun: The mix of adventure and chill vibes here is pure magic! ✨
