Manila Stays 🏙️

Days 1-3: Manila Kickoff (March 24-26) 🌟

Location: Near NAIA & Mall of Asia, Pasay City

Stay: Sheraton Manila Hotel or Hilton Manila - perfect spots near the airport and mall! ✈️🛍️

Activities: Land at 9 am on March 24, check in, and chill after your Dublin flight. Hit up Mall of Asia for shopping, dining, and soaking in the urban vibe. 🍔🛒

Unexpected Fun: Mall of Asia’s mix of global brands and local eats might surprise you - a cool urban kickoff before the beaches! 🌇

Festival Alert! 🎉 No major fests, but Holy Week prep might buzz around Pasay (March 30 - April 5). Check for mini barangay fiestas near Mall of Asia—think food stalls and music! 🥁

Search: Mall of Asia at Dusk
Day 26: Manila Finale (April 18) 🌅

Stay: Back at Sheraton Manila Hotel near the airport

Activities: Return from Cardona, relax, or do last-minute shopping at Mall of Asia before your 5 pm flight on April 19. 🛍️✈️

Vibe: Wrap up your epic trip with a chill day in the city! 😌
