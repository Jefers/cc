Bohol Bliss 🌿

Days 4-7: Bohol (March 26-29) 🌴

Transportation: Morning flight from Manila to Tagbilaran (~1 hr, Flight 1) on March 26. Book at Philippine Airlines ✈️

Stay: Bohol Beach Club or Panglao Island Nature Resort - beachfront heaven! 🏖️

Activities: Four nights (March 26-28):

  • Chocolate Hills - epic drone shots if allowed! 📸
  • Tarsier Sanctuary - meet the tiny cuties 🐒
  • Loboc River Cruise - chill vibes on the water 🚤
  • Panglao Island beaches - swim & relax 🌊

Unexpected Fun: Bohol’s mix of hills, tarsiers, and beaches is a surprising twist on a beach trip! 🌟

Festival Alert! 🎉 Tagbilaran’s big Sandugo Festival is in July, but March might bring a small barangay fiesta—ask locals for saint’s day celebrations near Chocolate Hills! 🥁
