Philippine Adventure Itinerary 🌴✈️

Overview 🌟

Your 27-day trip from March 24 to April 19, 2025, is packed with beaches, history, and city vibes! No flight limits, just pure fun.

Days 1-3: Manila Kickoff 🏙️ (March 24-26)

Location: Near NAIA & Mall of Asia

Stay: Sheraton Manila or Hilton Manila

Vibe: Chill after your flight from Dublin, shop & dine at Mall of Asia 🛍️

Days 4-8: El Nido, Palawan 🏝️ (March 26-30)

Travel: Flight to Puerto Princesa ✈️ (~1 hr), then 4-hr van (doable!)

Stay: El Nido Resort or Lagen Island Resort

Fun: Island hopping (Big & Small Lagoon), snorkeling, drone shots 📸

  • Tip: Check drone rules!
Days 9-13: Bohol 🌿 (March 31 - April 4)

Travel: Van to Puerto Princesa, fly to Tagbilaran ✈️ (~1 hr 20 min)

Stay: Bohol Beach Club or Panglao Island Nature Resort

Fun: Chocolate Hills, Tarsier Sanctuary, Panglao beaches 🏖️, Loboc River Cruise

Days 14-18: Cebu 🎉 (April 5-9)

Travel: Fly to Cebu City ✈️ (~30 min)

Stay: Shangri-La Mactan or Cebu City Marriott

Fun: Magellan's Cross, Fort San Pedro, Moalboal diving 🤿, city vibes

Days 19-25: Cardona Chill 🤝 (April 10-16)

Travel: Land to Cardona (~2 hrs)

Vibe: Stay with your friend, visit Daranak Falls 🌊 (6 days)

Note: Cut from 7 days to squeeze in more adventure!

Day 26: Manila Finale 🌇 (April 17)

Stay: Back to Sheraton Manila

Vibe: Last-minute shopping or chill before your 5 pm flight on April 18 ✈️

Travel Tips 🚀

  • Book flights early at Philippine Airlines ✈️
  • Summer vibes (March-April), but watch for storms 🌧️
  • Drone rules: Check local regs for epic shots 📸
