Drone Photography Tips 📸

Get those epic shots, but play it safe:

  • Check Philippine Civil Aviation Regulations - no flying near airports or sensitive spots! ✈️
  • Ask locals in El Nido, Coron, Bohol, and Cebu for specific rules 🗣️
  • Best spots: Big Lagoon (El Nido), Chocolate Hills (Bohol) - pure gold from above! 🌴

Extra Tips 🌟

Flight Schedules: Book early for March 26, March 30, April 3, and April 10 at Philippine Airlines ✈️

Weather: March-April is summer - perfect for beaches, but watch for storms! 🌧️

Budget: Resorts can get pricey - negotiate van rides for deals! 💸

Unexpected Fun: Cebu’s history-beach combo might blow your mind! ✨

Festival Alert! 🎉 You’ll catch Holy Week (March 30 - April 5) in Cebu & El Nido, plus possible local fiestas everywhere! Side trip idea: Moriones Festival in Marinduque (Holy Week) - Roman soldier masks and drama! 🎭
