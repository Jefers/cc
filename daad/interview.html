Funnel vs. Website

This approach is different from your website.

  • Your website is your digital brochure.
  • This funnel will be your #1 sales tool.

Part 1: The "Transformation Call" Funnel

"To build this, I need to understand a few key things about your business. Let's start with your ideal client and your offer."

Define: Client & Offer

  • Who is your ideal new client from Instagram?
  • What is their single biggest frustration or goal?
  • What is your most popular or profitable service for a new client?

Craft: Your "Free Gift"

  • If you had 15 minutes to teach them one thing for a quick win, what would it be?
  • We will film a short, high-value video of you teaching this. This video is the core of the entire system.

Define: Goal & Proof

  • After they watch your free training, what is the one action you want them to take? (e.g., Book a free call).
  • Do you have any client testimonials, before/after photos, or success stories?

Part 2: How The Funnel Works

"Here is the simple, step-by-step system we will build based on your answers."

Steps 1 & 2

  • Instagram Hook: Create content with a clear call-to-action sending people to the "link in bio".
  • Landing Page: A simple page with a powerful headline, your free training video, and a large, unmissable button to book a call.

Steps 3 & 4

  • Seamless Booking: The button links to your calendar (Calendly) showing availability for the next 2-3 days to create urgency.
  • Capture Leads: A hidden Facebook Pixel will allow us to retarget visitors who didn't book a call.

Part 3: Future Revenue

"Once this funnel is working, we can expand your revenue with other income streams."

Expansion Ideas

  • Sell Digital Guides: Low-priced PDFs for followers not ready for 1-on-1 coaching.
  • "Book Funnel": Offer a free physical book (they pay shipping) and upsell to an online course.
  • Email Nurturing: Run special promotions and offers to your growing email list.

Executive Summary

"This plan moves you from a passive presence to an active client-acquisition system. It's a proven, pragmatic method for generating leads and revenue for a service-based business like yours."
