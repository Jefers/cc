🧠 Hunger Management (The 1,400 kcal Survival Guide)

1. The Sodium "Cheat Code"

At 10:30am, drink ½ tsp Pink Salt in 300ml warm water. Cuts cravings within 10 minutes.

2. Air Fryer Skin Trick

Salt thighs 30 min before cooking. Skin puffs = more chewing, more satiety.

3. Bone Broth Appetizer

Hot broth + salt 10 min before dinner. Reduces portion need.

4. Caffeine Timing

First coffee 9:30am, second (half-caf) 2pm. Smooths hunger curve.
