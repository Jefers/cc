2

Before starting

Do these first. This avoids most problems.

A

Use one earbud

Plug in one earbud or one headphone. Use a wired earbud if possible. Bluetooth can work, but wired is usually easier.

You hear PC music and alerts. The room does not hear them.
B

Turn speakers off

Turn down or unplug room speakers. Do not let music come out loud in the room.

If the webcam mic hears speakers, echo and feedback can return.
C

Play music on the PC

Use Spotify, YouTube, or another music app on the PC.

Do not use phone music. Phone music becomes room sound.
3

Download OBS Studio

1

Open the OBS website

Go to:

obsproject.com

Choose Windows.

2

Install OBS

Open the downloaded installer. Keep the normal default options. Finish the install.

3

Open OBS

If OBS asks to auto-configure, choose streaming. Start simple: 1280×720 and 30 FPS.

4

Get the Stripchat stream key

The stream key connects OBS to Stripchat. Treat it like a password.

  1. Log in to Stripchat.
  2. Open the Broadcast Center.
  3. Tap or click Set Up Broadcast.
  4. Choose External Broadcast Software or Switch to External Broadcast Software (OBS).
  5. Tap or click Show OBS Broadcasting Specification.
  6. Copy the Server / RTMP URL.
  7. Copy the Stream Key / Token / Broadcast Token.
Never show the stream key on camera. Never send it to customers. It controls your live broadcast connection.
5

Set up OBS Studio

Do this once. After it works, the daily routine is much easier.

Connect OBS to Stripchat
  1. Open OBS.
  2. Go to Settings → Stream.
  3. For Service, choose Custom.
  4. Paste the Stripchat Server / RTMP URL into the Server box.
  5. Paste the Stripchat Stream Key / Token into the Stream Key box.
  6. Click Apply, then OK.
Make one clean scene
  1. Find the Scenes box in OBS.
  2. Click the + button.
  3. Name the scene: Stripchat Clean Audio.
  4. Use this one scene until the audio is working perfectly.
Add webcam video
  1. Find the Sources box.
  2. Click +.
  3. Choose Video Capture Device.
  4. Name it: Webcam Video.
  5. Select the Logitech webcam, for example Logitech C922.
  6. Recommended start: 1280×720, 30 FPS.
  7. If there is a webcam audio option here, leave it off. Add the mic separately in the next step.
Add webcam mic voice
  1. In Sources, click +.
  2. Choose Audio Input Capture.
  3. Name it: Webcam Mic — Voice.
  4. Select the Logitech webcam microphone.
  5. Talk normally. The Webcam Mic — Voice meter should move.
  6. Do not add another microphone unless needed.
Add PC music
  1. In Sources, click +.
  2. Choose Audio Output Capture.
  3. Name it: PC Music — Desktop Audio.
  4. Select the same output device used for the earbud/headphones. If unsure, choose Default.
  5. Play music on the PC. The PC Music meter should move in OBS.

Important: This is how customers hear music without the music playing in the room.

Turn off duplicate audio
  1. Go to Settings → Audio.
  2. For Global Audio Devices, turn off anything not needed.
  3. A simple setup should show only two useful meters in OBS: Webcam Mic — Voice and PC Music — Desktop Audio.
  4. If the same sound appears twice, mute or remove the duplicate source.

This prevents echo and doubled sound.

6

Set the sound levels

The meters in OBS tell you what viewers will hear.

🎤

Voice meter

Talk normally. The Webcam Mic — Voice meter should move. It should not stay red.

Goal: voice is clear and louder than the music.
🎵

Music meter

Play music on the PC. The PC Music meter should move. Lower it if it covers your voice.

Goal: music is pleasant, not overpowering.
🔇

Mute button

In the OBS Audio Mixer, click the speaker icon beside Webcam Mic — Voice.

Red X = muted. No red X = viewers can hear you.
7

Test before going live

Best test: click Start Recording in OBS, talk for 20 seconds, play music, stop recording, then listen. Do this before starting the show.
8

Go live routine

Use the same order every time.

1

Open OBS

Open the scene named Stripchat Clean Audio.

2

Check sound

Room speakers off. One earbud in. Music on PC. Watch both OBS meters.

3

Open Stripchat

Open Broadcast Center and use External Broadcast Software / OBS mode.

4

Start OBS

Click Start Streaming in OBS.

5

Start Stripchat

Return to Stripchat and click Start Show or Go Live.

6

Use mic mute

When not speaking, mute Webcam Mic — Voice. Keep music playing.

The rules that make money

Always do this

  • ✓Use music from the PC.
  • ✓Wear one earbud.
  • ✓Keep voice clean and clear.
  • ✓Mute the mic when not speaking.

Never do this

  • ×Do not play phone music out loud.
  • ×Do not use room speakers during the stream.
  • ×Do not let viewers hear music through the webcam mic.
  • ×Do not ignore robot voice or feedback.
10

If something goes wrong

🎵

Viewers cannot hear music

Make sure music is playing on the PC. In OBS, the PC Music — Desktop Audio meter must move. If it does not move, choose a different device in the Audio Output Capture source.

🎤

Viewers cannot hear your voice

Make sure the Webcam Mic — Voice meter moves when you talk. Make sure the mic is not muted with a red X.

🔁

Echo or doubled sound

There is probably more than one mic or more than one desktop-audio source active. Keep only Webcam Mic — Voice and PC Music — Desktop Audio.

🤖

Robot voice

Turn room speakers off first. Then disable extra noise suppression or audio enhancements in Logitech software, Windows, or the browser if needed.

📷

Camera is black in OBS

Close other apps that may be using the camera, including browser preview tabs or Logitech camera software. Reopen OBS after closing them.

🔑

OBS will not connect to Stripchat

Check the RTMP URL and Stream Key/Token. Copy them again from Stripchat and paste them into OBS → Settings → Stream.

Final simple promise

Clean audio makes the stream easier to watch. Easier to watch means customers stay longer.

Talk into the webcam mic. Play music on the PC. Hear it in one earbud. Keep room speakers off. Mute the mic when not speaking.

Button names can change slightly. Look for similar words: External Broadcast Software, OBS, RTMP URL, Stream Key, Token, Start Show, or Go Live.
