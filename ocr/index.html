How to use:

  1. Take a picture using your camera or upload an image from your device
  2. Click "Extract Text" to process the image
  3. Wait for the text extraction to complete
  4. Select the text you want to copy from the text area
  5. Click "Copy Selected Text" to copy to clipboard
