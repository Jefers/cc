How to use this Diary

  • Tap the Title or Subtitle to change the text. It saves automatically!
  • Tap any photo to view it full screen, share it, or delete it.
  • The Center Camera Button opens your phone's camera directly.

Where do my photos go?
They are securely saved to your local browser storage. If you want to keep a photo in your device's Camera Roll permanently, tap the photo and use the Share button!
